The Venom Stealer MaaS platform poses a significant threat to individuals and organizations, as it enables the widespread distribution of malicious attacks that can evade traditional security measures.

Moreover, the automated nature of the platform allows threat actors to launch multiple attacks simultaneously, increasing the potential impact and reach of their malicious activities.
