The cybersecurity community is paying tribute to Tim Wilson, co-founder and former editor-in-chief of Dark Reading, who passed away five years ago. As Dark Reading enters its third decade, it's an opportunity to reflect on Wilson's instrumental role in building and elevating the media site. His legacy continues to inspire and influence the cybersecurity industry, with a lasting impact on the way cybersecurity news and information are consumed and shared.
